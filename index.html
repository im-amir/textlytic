How it works

We Receive Urdu Audio Calls Data

The calls are in the form of audio files. They can be large or small files, so we divide the large files into small chunks of files. They come from call centers in real time. These calls are then sent to the backend and using GStreamer, we send them to Kaldi Modal that has been trained on thousands of calls already.

Urdu Calls Converted To Text Using Kaldi

Next the calls are sent from GSteamer to Kaldi and here Urdu calls are converted to text through automatic speech recognition stage. At this stage, the kaldi modal kicks in the place to leverage advanced machine learning models to convert the urdu audio calls into urdu text for better and quick analysis.
Converted calls are automatically sent by GStreamer worker to the backend server and we receive translated text in a presented form back at Dashboard.
